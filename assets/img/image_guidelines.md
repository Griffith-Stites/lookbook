# Image Guidelines

## Look book
* Detail images: 3:4 aspect ratio as JPG 50% quality
  * Should be 1170x1560
* Cover Images: 3:4 aspect ratio as JPG 50% quality
  * Should be 575x767
* Can utilize the Photoshop Image Processor Script
