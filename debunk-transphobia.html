Still Think Trans Women Have Male Privilege? These 7 Points Prove They Don’t

I do not agree with all of the things the author has to say, but they bring up good points in explaining why trans women do not have male privilege.
My personal view is that I have experienced some privilege and opportunities by living the first 18 years of my life identifying and presenting as a man, though I think this experience is distinctly different than male privilege. I internalized societal messages directed at women as applying to myself. My experience being treated as male was extremely different than those of a cisgender boy. I was terrified and felt awful being treated this way, instead of being empowered by it.
Regardless of privilege, solidarity between all women - and trans women are women - is important and necessary.
