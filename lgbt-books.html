LGBTQ+ Flag Books

I wrote these short books for use in the Flag Quest video game. Flag Quest is a rogue like game created to spread awareness of LGBTQ+ identities by integrating the elements of LGBTQ+ flags into fun puzzles. I helped to develop this game with a team of three other Olin students.


I could not have written these books without the help from countless Internet resources.
