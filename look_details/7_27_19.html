Queer

When I first went out in public with this look, I like a pretty girl for the first time ever. I did not feel pretty despite being trans. I did not feel pretty despite being assigned male at birth. I felt attractive, and like an attractive queer woman.

What is so amazing about the combination of the romper, jewelry, boots, and a little makeup is how powerful it makes me feel. This outfit is very different from most of the other outfits I usually wear. It is all black and is definetly more aggressive than my other looks. The outfit conforms less to heteronormative ideas of what makes a woman beautiful. But those differences are what allow me to bring my true self. Wearing this outfit is completely for me, and is not something that in anyway conforms to what society says I should do. Wearing this outfit makes me feel like the queer woman I know I am.
