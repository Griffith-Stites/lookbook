Pink Spring Formal

Pink Spring Floral

I recently started an internship in Fargo and wanted to go out and about in something floral in fun. I am wearing the first dress I ever purchased, the second dress I ever put on. It is still my favorite dress to this day (plus it is reversible).

A big reason I like this dress is because of my positive experience purchasing the dress. I was with my boyfriend at the mall and walked past White House Black Market. I was terrified to go in. When I went into the store, an employee asked me "What is the occasion?" even though I appeared as a man just in sweats and a hoodie. This was such a simple question but made it clear she was here to support. The first two dresses I tried on almost left me in tears. They made me feel so good about my body. The employees there were incredibly supportive and said I looked great in them. I could not have asked for a better first dress shopping experience.

Wearing floral makes me feel beautiful and can be a great confidence booster when I am feeling dysphoric. I love the way this fits around my shoulders. It makes my shoulders look narrow and does not grab at my arms too tightly. I also like how the dress fits flat against my chest and stomach.The addition of the included belt makes the the dress flair out around my hips, giving me a little curve.

It was great to get out and about in Fargo as my true self. Fargo does not have a very visible LGBT/trans community and I want to do my part to support trans visibility!
