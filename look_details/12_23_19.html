Flowy Jumpsuit

Flowy Jumpsuit

This is the first time I have warn a shoulderless outfit since coming out as trans. This represents a powerful moment for me, because I am revealing a non-traditionally feminine part of my body: my shoulders. Confidentially wearing this out in public, while on vacation, helped me realize how much confidence I have gained since coming out

I have overcome so much messaging on what a woman is supposed to look like. This semester, I spent a lot of time in queer women and nonbinary spaces. Spending so much time around powerful people who defied gender norms helped me realize that I was a part of that community too. I can too be beautiful and desirable non conforming to gender norsms. That queer and trans is not something to hide, but something to be proud of.
