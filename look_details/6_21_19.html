Van Gogh

Van Gogh

Despite being a dress I almost did not purchase (as it is a bit too tight under the arms), this dress I got at Savers has become one of my favorites. It was the dress I was wearing when someone first called me "Miss" in public. It was such an amazing feeling to know someone I did not know did not see me as a man (plus, I think he may have been flirting with me a little).

I love how the dress makes me look not completely flat chested. And despite it being my lowest cut dress, I feel quite comfortable in it. Wearing this dress and getting called "Miss" (now multiple times) has really validated me as a transgender person.
