People of Color Books

 It is important to take an intersectional approach to our activism. This is especially true within LGBT spaces, as queer spaces are very focused on the issues of white, middle class people.

 I also feel that (as a white queer trans person) learning how to be a better ally to people of color has helped me better understand my own issues. In particular, learning about people of color allyship helped me to communicate with cisgender people about how to be a better trans ally.

 I want to be clear that I am by no means an expert on people of color issues. I am white and still have a lot of room to grow. I highly encourage other white queer people to learn about people of color issues; as well as making an effort to follow people of color activists and participate in social justice movements focused on the troubles which most effect people of color.
