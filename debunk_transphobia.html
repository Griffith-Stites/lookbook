Get out of Your Head

Resources to debunk transphobic arguments. Meant to help you fight internal transphobic voices and validate yourself in your gender identity.

.

Counter Trans Erasure

'Rapid Onset Gender Dysphoria'

Why 'Rapid-onset gender dysphoria' is bad science

Explains the flawed logic behind the "proof" for 'Rapid-onset gender dysphoria'.

Everything You Need to Know About Rapid Onset Gender Dysphoria

Explains the history and invention of the term. Gives examples - in a trans and dysphoria context - of how correlation does not equal causation. It also talks about why trans and other LGBT people tend to bond and come out together.


'Gender Critical' Viewpoints

Thoughts about Transphobia TERFs and TUMFs

Discusses the different types of transphobia and helps to provide distinctions between different transphobic positions. Also helps to explain that many transphobic feminists are not TERFs, and just are trans-unaware. This provided me hope for the future of trans rights and feminism.

Why Sexual Assault is not an Excuse for Transphobia: A Response to ‘Gender Critical Feminists’

Explains why being a sexual assault is not an excuse for being transphobic. This includes why it is innappropriate to misgender trans people, particularly trans women, and why not having relations with a trans woman simply because she has a penis is wrong and transphobic.

Is Medium Transphobic?

A bit of a rant where the author discusses the infultration of transphobic bloggers onto multiple platforms. She also talks about the female penis and takes on a number of 'gender critical' viewpoints.

The “T” in “#MeToo”

Explains the often ignored issue of disproportionately high sexual violence that transgender people face.



Trans Children

Why This Sudden Trans-Kids Trend?

Explains why so many kids are now coming out as being trans. Helps to explain that this is not a trend, but in fact a result of more acceptance and less fear in our society.

Detransition, Desistance, and Disinformation: A Guide for Understanding Transgender Children Debates

Does a great job explaining the systemic transphobia that is often ignored by mainstream cisgender journalists which claim to be trans supportive. Also does a great job giving examples of how trans bodies/lives are valued as lesser than cis bodies/lives. Uses this information to give a nuanced answer to how we should handle gender dysphoria and trans issues in children.

An Open Letter to the Tween Girl's Clothing Store, Justice

Wholesome story from a parent of a 10-year-old gender non-conforming son.
