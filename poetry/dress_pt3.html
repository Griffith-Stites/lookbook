Dress: Part III



 

Flowing floral gown

First true feeling of beauty

Tears flowing on cheeks

 

Smiling ear to ear

Rushing water breaks levee

Flood of emotions

 

Commentary: This poem was inspired by my first time trying on a dress. Thankfully, the first dress I tried on fit me perfectly. I was struck in awe by the way it made me feel. I was struggling to hold back tears and the amount of gender euphoria at that moment was the greatest in my life. It made me feel beautiful for the first time ever.
