Not a Man




One-legged,

One-eyed,

He bounces aboard his bedroom ship.

 

Draws a cutlass,

Gives out a theatrical ARRRR,

"Walk the plank yee scallywag".

 

In pursuit of booty,

Sailing across the ocean blue,

He darts down the hallway.

 

Lighting flashes,

Thunder sounds,

He plays pretend with his voice as an aide.

 

Commentary: Writing this poem caused a lot of mental anguish. It was based on both internal and external forces telling me that I am just a man pretending to be something else for some stupid reason. The external forces include people who are trans exclusive and see me being AMAB and trans as some sort of threat or invasion of space. Some people see me as some sort of predator.

 

This message then becomes internalized, and I am trying to constantly fight it. I know it not to be true, but at times I definitely feeling like I am just some fucked up man pretending to be something I am not.
